CoursesWe have

  • AI & IOT

    Learn how Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things work together to automate systems, control devices, and analyze data. Explore smart home projects, IoT sensors, and AI decision-making through real-time applications.

  • Robotics & Embedded Systems

    Master robot design, sensor integration, motor control, and embedded systems programming. Build your own robots like line-followers, obstacle avoiders, and smart security bots.

  • Scratch Programming

    An engaging, block-based coding platform for creating games, animations, and interactive stories. Best for introducing young minds to programming logic.

  • Arduino & ESP8266 Projects

    Hands-on projects using Arduino Nano and ESP8266 Wi-Fi boards. Create wireless control systems, home automation setups, alarms, and Bluetooth-operated devices.

  • Python Basics

    Learn the world’s most popular beginner-friendly programming language. Cover variables, loops, decision-making, and build basic apps and programs.

  • Flutter App Development

    Design and develop mobile apps for Android and iOS. Learn UI creation, animations, navigation, and link apps with hardware-based IoT projects.

  • Abacus & Speed Maths

    Boost mental calculation speed, memory, and focus with traditional Abacus techniques and modern speed maths shortcuts.

  • Personality Development

    Build self-confidence, leadership, communication skills, problem-solving, and stage presence through interactive workshops and public speaking sessions.

  • Spoken English

    Improve fluency, grammar, vocabulary, conversation, and accent in an easy, interactive manner for students of all levels.

  • Chess

    Learn basic to advanced chess strategies, problem-solving techniques, and competitive skills through practical chess coaching.
